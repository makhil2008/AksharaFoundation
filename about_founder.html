Mr. Manepalli Kotaiah

CEO & Founder

"Empower children. Bring Communities together."

Mr. Manepalli Kotaiah is a native of Avanigadda village in Krishna District. He had his schooling from Sisu Vidya Mandiram during 1970 to 1977 and high schooling from Govt. Junior college, Avanigadda from 1977 to 1980. He has completed Intermediate from Hindu College in Machilipatnam during 1980 to 1982.

He is a Gold Medallist in BE (Civil) 1982-1986 from Andhra University and M.Tech (Structures) from Anna University-1986-88. He has served in pivotal positions such as Lecturer at KITS, Warangal, Chief Engineer at Nuclear Power Corporation of India Ltd. (DAE), Advisor to the Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant in Bangladesh.

He is currently working as Vice President (Nuclear) at MEIL, Hyderabad. He is also a member of the Academic Council of SRKR Engineering College, offering his expertise and guidance to foster academic and professional growth. His global exposure includes technical deputations to Moscow, Paris, and Bangladesh. He is having over 36 years of diverse experience in all the fields of Civil Engineering.

He started a social service organization named "Akshara Foundation" at AVANIGADDA to support and promote the education for the children of AVANIGADDA (his native village) and neighboring villages.
