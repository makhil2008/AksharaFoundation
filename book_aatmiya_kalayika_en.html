A welcoming space in Avanigadda for family reunions, community events, and cultural gatherings—free for all registered members.

Welcome to Aatmiya Kalayika – Your Venue for Meaningful Gatherings

Akshara Foundation proudly offers a spacious, fully-equipped facility in the heart of Avanigadda, designed for community events, social service activities, educational workshops, and family reunions. Whether you're planning a quiet gathering or a vibrant event, Aatmiya Kalayika provides a comfortable, homely space to bring people together.

Facility Highlights

  • Spacious 2000 sq. ft. Hall – Ideal for large family or community events
  • Two Private Rooms – Includes attached toilets for added privacy and comfort
  • Equipped Kitchen – Perfect for cooking or catering during gatherings
  • Centrally Located – Situated in the heart of AVANIGADDA, easy to reach
  • Built for Connection – A place to reconnect, learn, and create memories

Facility Features

  • Hall - Our air-conditioned hall accommodates up to 75 guests, ideal for family reunions, cultural programs, community meetings, or workshops.
  • Private Rooms - Two private rooms are available—each with an attached toilet. These are perfect for rest, privacy, or smaller group discussions during events.
  • Kitchen - The on-site kitchen is fully equipped, allowing families to prepare and enjoy meals together—just like home.
  • Other Amenities - It also includes 4 clean, common toilets, ample parking space and easy accessibility for all guests.

Terms & Conditions

To ensure smooth operations and fairness for all:

  • Respect the allotted event duration and timings
  • Maximum occupancy is 75 people
  • You are responsible for clean-up and maintenance after use
  • Food prep and catering must follow basic hygiene and safety rules

Please review the full set of guidelines before your booking is confirmed.

Booking the Facility

This venue is available free of charge to all registered members of Aatmiya Kalayika. Our goal is to make it easier for families and communities to come together and celebrate, learn, and grow.
