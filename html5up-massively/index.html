At Akshara Foundation, our mission is two-fold: to empower individuals through education and to foster meaningful human connections. We are dedicated to creating supportive spaces where families can reconnect with their roots, strengthen bonds, and build lasting memories together. At the same time, we remain firmly committed to promoting education and personal development by offering programs that nurture learning and unlock potential. Through this holistic approach, Akshara Foundation strives to give back to the community—not only by uplifting minds but also by restoring and deepening relationships.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Manepalli Kotaiah

Founder and CEO

"Empower children. Bring Communities together."
A native of Avanigadda in Krishna District, is a distinguished Civil Engineer with over 36 years of experience. A Gold Medallist in BE (Civil) from Andhra University and M.Tech (Structures) from Anna University, he has held key positions including Chief Engineer at NPCIL and Advisor to the Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant in Bangladesh. Currently, he serves as Vice President (Nuclear) at MEIL, Hyderabad, and is a member of the Academic Council at SRKR Engineering College. With global exposure in Moscow, Paris, and Bangladesh, he brings a wealth of expertise. Driven by a passion for community service, he founded Akshara Foundation to support education in Avanigadda and nearby villages

Aatmiya kalayika

Aatmiya Kalayika is a heartfelt initiative based in Avanigadda, created to help individuals and families reconnect with their roots, loved ones, and native traditions. Designed as a community hall and guest space, it offers a welcoming, fully-equipped facility—free of charge for registered members—for family reunions, cultural celebrations, educational events, and social gatherings. Through this space, Aatmiya Kalayika fosters togetherness, nostalgia, and a renewed sense of belonging for those connected to Diviseema and its surrounding villages.

Educational Programs

Akshara Foundation’s educational initiatives are dedicated to empowering individuals in the Avanigadda through practical learning, skills development, and community-based training. From workshops and academic support to vocational training and awareness sessions, these programs are designed to uplift students, youth, and lifelong learners alike. Held at the Aatmiya Kalayika facility and other local venues, the Foundation ensures that education is accessible, inclusive, and deeply rooted in the needs of the community.
